About me

Hi there!

I graduated in August 2017 from the University of Washington earning a Bachelor of Arts in Earth and Space Science and a minor in Computer Science and Software Engineering. I have a great interest in the environment, technology, and data. I worked as a Student Technology Assistant for the Communications Department at UW from May 2015 to June 2017. In September 2016 I earned Associate Level Certification as an AWS Solutions Architect. Below are some of the projects I've been working on.

Projects
Web Development with PHP and D3.
Uses PHP to store cookies, login, and data about a user's todo list. Todo list weights tasks with points based on due date, importance, and personal gratification of performing the task. Creates a data visualization of the points accumulated after completing tasks. Future work includes creating a game to spend the accumulated points.
GIS - Goldfinch Breeding Grounds in Oregon
Used GIS to generate maps of the American Goldfinch's breeding grounds. Performed different analyses including boolean raster manipulations to find relationships and patterns such as proximity to highways, certain vegetation, and streams.
Computer Simulation of Epidemic Modeling in Python
Led a team to research, design, develop, and present a computer simulation of an epidemic spreading across a school campus based on their class schedule and campus layout. Used agent-based modeling with discrete-space scheduled walkers. Developed in Python.
Interactive Tsunami History Visualization
Uses the D3.js framework to display data about tsunami's around the world. Has adjustable sliders to interactively select a subset of the data based on year, earthquake magnitude, and cause of tsunami.
Huky Wushu Team - Web Development
Led a team to update the website for the Husky Wushu Team. Created a responsive, mobile friendly UI using Bootstrap. Used PHP to create contact messages in-site.
